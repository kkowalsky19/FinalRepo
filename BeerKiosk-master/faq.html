Beer Kiosk

FAQ

  • What can I get to drink?
    • The bar decides exactly what drink is being served. We currently only support beer, but plan to add support mixed drinks in the future.
  • How do I order?
    • Go the main site page, and choose how many beers you'd like, then enter your phone number and credit card information. If the transaction was approved, then you will receive your codes via text. Enter the codes at the kiosk and the drink will be dispensed right after.
